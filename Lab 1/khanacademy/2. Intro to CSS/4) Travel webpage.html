Travel to Semey

Semey is the historical and cultural center of Eastern Kazakhstan, offering a journey through the ages. Located on the banks of the Irtysh River, this city is rich in history, culture, and architectural landmarks.

Main Attractions:

  1. Unique 1086-meter Suspension Bridge

    One of the longest pedestrian bridges in Kazakhstan, offering stunning views of the Irtysh River.
  2. Dostoevsky Literary Memorial Museum
    Dedicated to the famous Russian writer Fyodor Dostoevsky, who spent five years in Semey during his exile.
  3. Abay Museum-Reserve
    A cultural complex dedicated to Abay Kunanbayev, the great Kazakh poet and philosopher.
  4. Victory Park and the Picturesque Embankment of the Irtysh River
    Perfect for leisurely walks, with monuments, fountains, and beautiful views of the river.

The best time to visit Semey is from May to September when the weather is warm and pleasant, or in winter for those who appreciate snowy landscapes and winter activities.
